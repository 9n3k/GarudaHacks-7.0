Microphone Access Needed

WaspadaOjol cannot monitor nearby vehicles without microphone permission.

Why is it needed?

  • 🚗 Detect approaching vehicles
  • 🔊 Trigger AI voice warnings
  • 📳 Activate vibration alerts
  • 🛡️ Keep you safer while walking
⚠️ Microphone is only used while Street Mode is active and is never active on the Home page.
